Original Soul Food

Taste the soul in Rusty Bucket full of Fish Heads

By : Mama Sugarback's Soul Food Shack

Backstory

Mama Sugarback's fish-head bucket is one of her original 13 recipes. This recipe was originally discoverd by Mama Sugarback when she lived in the swamp, eating nothing but grilled donkey teeth lathered in honey lard. She discovered it on accident on a fishing trip while gutting the fish she caught and realized that this could be the food that could fullfil her life-long dream of opening her own restaurant.

Ingredients:

  • 5 Fish Heads(Can be more depening on how many enjoyers)
  • Rusty Bucket
  • Fish Blood
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions

  1. Gut the fish and disgard of the contents and collect all of the blood
  2. Next, remove all of the fish meat while leaving the head alone
  3. Place all fish heads inside rusty bucket
  4. Place all blood into the bucket then mix
